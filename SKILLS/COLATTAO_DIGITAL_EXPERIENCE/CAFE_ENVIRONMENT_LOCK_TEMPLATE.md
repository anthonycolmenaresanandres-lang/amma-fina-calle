# Cafe Environment Lock Template

## Purpose

Lock the Colattao cafe environment before generating owner-led or Digital Menu campaign visuals.

```txt
CAFE NAME:
[Colattao / client cafe]

APPROVED INTERIOR REFERENCE IMAGES:
- [path/reference 1]
- [path/reference 2]
- [path/reference 3]

COUNTER LAYOUT:
[Counter position, materials, visible equipment, customer-facing angle]

WALL COLOR/MATERIAL:
[Paint, wood, tile, brick, art, shelves]

LIGHTING TEMPERATURE:
[Warm espresso, daylight, tungsten, mixed, practical lamps]

MENU PLACEMENT:
[Where Digital Menu or menu board appears if approved]

OWNER POSITION:
[Where owner/spokesperson stands or sits]

CUSTOMER POSITION:
[Where customers appear, if used]

PROPS:
[Approved props: cups, pastries, counter items, QR stand, menu card]

FORBIDDEN CHANGES:
[No different cafe, no fake signage, no fake menu text, no invented logo, no unrelated decor]

APPROVED CAMERA ANGLES:
[Front counter, 3/4 counter, over-shoulder, close-up, etc.]

MOBILE READABILITY NOTES:
[Where to leave space for logo, QR, Digital Menu overlay, captions]
```

## Rule

Do not render production campaign video inside a cafe environment until this lock is approved.
