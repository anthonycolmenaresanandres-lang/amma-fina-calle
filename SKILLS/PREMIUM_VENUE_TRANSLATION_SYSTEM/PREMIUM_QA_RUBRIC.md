# Premium QA Rubric

## Purpose

Use this rubric to score whether a venue campaign fits the Fina Calle premium system while preserving the venue's identity.

Score each category from 1 to 5.

- 1 = fails standard.
- 2 = weak / risky.
- 3 = acceptable but needs refinement.
- 4 = strong.
- 5 = premium system-ready.

## Rubric

| Category | Score | What To Check |
|---|---:|---|
| Brand prestige | 1-5 | Does the campaign feel high-end, intentional, and venue-specific? |
| Visual consistency | 1-5 | Do shots, colors, overlays, and motion feel like one campaign? |
| Environment fit | 1-5 | Does the setting match the venue and approved environment references? |
| Owner credibility | 1-5 | If owner appears, does identity feel approved, realistic, and trustworthy? |
| Product elegance | 1-5 | Are food/drink hero items appealing without clutter or distortion? |
| Overlay cleanliness | 1-5 | Are logos, QR codes, CTA text, and menu elements clean overlays? |
| Mobile readability | 1-5 | Does the campaign read clearly on 9:16 mobile screens? |
| Campaign cohesion | 1-5 | Does the sequence move from hook to product/Digital Menu proof to CTA cleanly? |

## Passing Threshold

Minimum recommended launch score:

```txt
TOTAL SCORE: 32 / 40 minimum
NO CATEGORY BELOW: 4 for final production
```

If any category scores below 4, revise before production.

## Automatic Fail Conditions

Fail the campaign if:

- Logo is AI-generated or mutated.
- QR code is AI-generated or unverified.
- CTA text is AI-generated or misspelled.
- Digital Menu copy is AI-generated or inaccurate.
- Menu prices/items are invented.
- Owner identity is used without approval.
- Venue environment is used without approval.
- The campaign looks generic or copied from Colattao instead of translated.
- Mobile readability fails.
