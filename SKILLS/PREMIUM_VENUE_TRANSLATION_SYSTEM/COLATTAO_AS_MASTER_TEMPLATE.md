# Colattao As Master Template

## Purpose

Colattao is the Fina Calle OS reference case for premium local restaurant campaigns. It should guide structure, discipline, and quality, but it should not be cloned visually for every restaurant.

## What Colattao Teaches

### Digital Menu Positioning

- The Digital Menu is treated as a premium brand experience, not a generic menu page.
- Digital Menu visuals are proof assets.
- Menu copy is controlled and must not be hallucinated.
- For Colattao campaign language, use Digital Menu, never website.

### Cafe Rush Promotion

- The game is a brand engagement layer.
- The campaign can promote both the Digital Menu and Cafe Rush without feeling like a gimmick.
- Game CTA artwork is an overlay, not a generated text element.

### Overlay Discipline

- Colattao logo, Fina Calle mark, CTA buttons, QR codes, footer signatures, and menu text stay outside AI generation.
- Generated stills or video should create atmosphere, motion, product presence, and premium framing.
- Final assembly handles readable elements.

### Product Sticker Usage

- Product stickers are lightweight, repeatable campaign accents.
- Stickers should support the product story without cluttering the frame.
- Stickers work best as small motion accents, menu callouts, or transition elements.

### Background Strategy

- Use approved 9:16 backgrounds for mobile-first launch content.
- Keep espresso/gold/premium cues controlled.
- Use environment references to create continuity, not random cafe scenes.

### Premium Campaign Structure

A Colattao-style campaign usually includes:

1. Premium visual hook.
2. Product or atmosphere reveal.
3. Digital Menu proof.
4. Cafe Rush or QR interaction cue.
5. Clean CTA overlay.
6. Brand signature/end-card.

## What Transfers To Other Venues

Transfer:

- Approval gates.
- Overlay discipline.
- 9:16 mobile-first standard.
- Premium pacing.
- Product hero structure.
- Owner/environment lock process.
- QA rubric.
- Digital Menu proof moment.

Do not transfer blindly:

- Colattao colors.
- Cafe-specific heritage cues.
- Cafe Rush wording if the client has no game.
- Coffee-specific props.
- Colattao menu copy.
- Colattao owner/environment identity.

## Translation Principle

Use Colattao as the operating model, not the costume.

Each new restaurant should feel like itself at a higher standard, not like Colattao wearing a new logo.
