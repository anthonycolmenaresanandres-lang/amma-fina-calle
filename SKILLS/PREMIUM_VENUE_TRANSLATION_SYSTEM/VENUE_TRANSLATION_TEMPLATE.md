# Venue Translation Template

## Purpose

Use this template to translate the Fina Calle premium campaign system to a new restaurant or venue while preserving the venue's identity.

## Venue Translation Variables

These change per restaurant.

| Variable | Required Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Logo | Approved PNG/SVG | Overlay only. Never AI-generated. |
| Owner identity | Approved owner photo packet | Required before owner generation. |
| Food/drink hero items | Approved product images | Choose 1 to 3 anchor products. |
| Environment | Approved interior/exterior packet | Required before venue environment lock. |
| Heritage/cultural cues | Written notes and approved visuals | Use respectfully and specifically. |
| Brand colors | Approved palette or extracted references | Must fit premium system without flattening identity. |
| Audience tone | Owner/client notes | Defines copy and pacing. |
| CTA emphasis | Approved CTA text and destination | Overlay only. |

## Campaign Translation Questions

1. What makes this venue feel premium already?
2. What should stay unique to this venue?
3. What should be translated from Colattao's structure, not copied visually?
4. What product or dish becomes the hero?
5. What Digital Menu proof should appear?
6. What owner or staff presence is needed?
7. What environment details make the venue credible?
8. What cultural/heritage cues are approved and safe to use?
9. What should never appear in this venue's campaign?
10. What CTA should the viewer remember?

## Stop Rules

Stop if:

- Logo is not approved.
- QR destination is not verified.
- Digital Menu proof is not approved.
- Owner identity is needed but not approved.
- Venue environment is needed but not approved.
- Product hero asset is missing.
- The campaign depends on AI-generated readable text.
