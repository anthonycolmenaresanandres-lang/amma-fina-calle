## Lightweight Campaign Standard

Favor fast, mobile-first, reusable campaigns:

- QR signage.
- Digital Menu CTA.
- Mini-game skin or teaser.
- Sticker/social asset pack.
- Website concept section.

Avoid heavy systems until revenue and repeat demand prove the need.
