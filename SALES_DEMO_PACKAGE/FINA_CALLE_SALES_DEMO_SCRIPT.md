## 4. Restaurant-Owner Explanation (plain language)

- **"What is it?"** → "A digital menu plus a branded mini-game behind one QR code,
  and a private page where you update your own menu."
- **"Do my customers download anything?"** → "No. They scan and it opens in their browser."
- **"Do you collect my customers' personal info?"** → "No names, no emails, no payment
  info. Analytics, when included, are anonymous." (Verified: PRICING_AND_OFFER.md §5, OWNER_DEMO_SCRIPT_ES §4.)
- **"Who keeps it updated?"** → "You can, from your owner page — or we do small updates
  as part of monthly maintenance."
- **"Is this a website?"** → "It doesn't replace your website. It adds an in-store
  engagement layer most websites don't cover." (Verified: SALES_SCRIPT.md §6.)
