# Demo URLs & Talk Track

> Every URL below is owner-verified live (browser PASS, 2026-06-07) on `https://finacalleos.com`.
> For each: what it proves, what to say, and what NOT to promise.

---

## 1. `https://finacalleos.com`
- **What it proves:** Fina Calle OS is a real, live, SSL-secured platform (real domain, real company — AMMA Ventures, Virginia Beach).
- **Say:** "This is our platform. Real company, real domain, already live."
- **Don't promise:** Don't imply every link on it is a finished product for them. It's the home base.

## 2. `https://finacalleos.com/m/colattao`  ⭐ THE HERO
- **What it proves:** A **real client's live digital menu** — Colattao. Renders business name, categories, items with prices, hours, and promos. Real data (e.g. Espresso, Almond Croissant).
- **Say:** "This is a real client, live right now. Your customers scan one QR and land on a clean menu like this — branded for you. Items, prices, hours, promos."
- **Don't promise:** Don't claim live online ordering, payments, or a feedback box *on this page* — the live menu shows menu/hours/promos. (Feedback box & analytics are part of the package setup, not visible on this page today — see `FEATURE_STATUS_TABLE.md`.)

## 3. `https://finacalleos.com/penalty-shootout`  🎮 ENGAGEMENT
- **What it proves:** The branded **mini-game** layer is real and playable — pick a keeper (Street/Club/Pro), take 5 shots, GOAL/SAVED/MISS, score + rating. Mobile-first.
- **Say:** "Here's the kind of fun layer we add so customers engage instead of glancing and leaving. Take five shots." (Hand them the phone.)
- **Honest framing:** It's a **playable demo of the game engine** (game R&D). A client version would be skinned with *their* brand. Don't present it as Colattao's finished production game.
- **Don't promise:** Don't promise a specific custom game is already built for the prospect.
- *(Optional second game: `https://finacalleos.com/conquest` is another live playable game demo.)*

## 4. `https://finacalleos.com/owner/colattao`  🔐 OWNER CONTROL
- **What it proves:** Each owner gets a **private, secure sign-in** (email) to manage their own menu. This is the real auth gate; once signed in, an authorized owner can edit menu items, prices, and promos.
- **Say:** "This is your private door. You log in here to update your own menu — no calls, no waiting. Only you get in."
- **How to show it:** Show the **Owner Sign-In screen** and explain it. **Do not fake a login** — you won't have Colattao's credentials, and that's fine; the sign-in screen makes the point.
- **Don't promise:** Don't click through to a dashboard you can't access; don't imply you can log into a client's account on demand.

## 5. `https://finacalleos.com/rd`  🔬 CREDIBILITY (optional)
- **What it proves:** Fina Calle is actively building more — and labels research **as research** (honesty builds trust). Lists R&D tracks (e.g. PayBridge payment-options *research*, plus the Penalty Shootout game).
- **Say:** "And here's our roadmap — clearly marked as research, not promises."
- **Don't promise:** Nothing on `/rd` is a live product. **Especially do not say payments are live** — PayBridge is explicitly R&D and *not a live payment system*.

## ⛔ Do NOT demo: `https://finacalleos.com/customers`
- This is **Fina Calle's internal** customer/account registry (admin sign-in), and the page itself states it is **"not a secure admin portal yet."** It is **not** a prospect-facing feature. Skip it entirely in front of restaurant owners.

## Universal "what not to promise" (all demos)
Not live / not offered as-is — never present as ready:
- Payment processing, checkout, online ordering
- POS integration
- AI automation
- Customer personal-data collection
- Guaranteed sales or traffic
- A finished custom game/feature before it's scoped and built
