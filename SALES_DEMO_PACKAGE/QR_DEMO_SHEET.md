# QR / Demo Sheet (Print Layout)

> A printable single sheet the rep can hand over or scan from. Three verified live URLs.
> No unverified claims. Print: Letter/A4 portrait, three cards stacked.
> **To make the QR images:** paste each URL into any free QR generator (e.g. qr-code-generator.com)
> or, if you want a ready link, use `https://api.qrserver.com/v1/create-qr-code/?size=600x600&data=<URL>`.
> Then drop the QR into the box for each card before printing.

### What NOT to put on this sheet
- ❌ Payments / online ordering / POS / AI / "guaranteed sales"
- ❌ `/customers` (internal admin — never give this to a prospect)
- ❌ Any "coming soon" feature (feedback box, analytics) shown as live
