# Colattanini — Staff Playbook
_Operations · MVP, manual · Created 2026-06-07_

A 2‑minute guide for Colattao staff. The whole campaign is **manual and fast**:
look at the customer's phone, hand them the matching sticker. No app, no login,
no data entry. See `PRODUCT_MODULES/COLATTAO_COLATTANINI_COLLECTIBLE_CAMPAIGN.md`.

> The reward is **"today's Colattanini reward"** — give whatever the owner has set
> for today. Do not promise a specific prize unless the owner has confirmed it.

---

## The loop (what you do)

1. Customer **scans** the QR and **plays** a keeper level on their phone.
2. Customer **shows you the result screen** (the end‑of‑match score screen).
3. You **hand them the matching sticker** for that keeper:

| They played… | Give this sticker |
|---|---|
| **Street Keeper** | **Churro Chispa** (#1) |
| **Club Keeper** | **Coco Beach Blitzer** (#2) |
| **Pro Keeper** | **Cali Croissant Captain** (#3) |

4. Offer a **laminated collector sheet** (first time) so they can stick it on.
5. When they've got **all 3 stickers**, give **today's Colattanini reward.**

That's it. The sticker matches the **keeper they played** — not whether they won.

---

## How to verify (eye‑check only)
- Look for the **result/score screen** of Penalty Shootout (shows the keeper name
  and a "X / 5" score with a rating).
- The keeper name is at the top (Street / Club / Pro) — that tells you which sticker.
- **You do not need them to have scored or won** — playing the keeper is enough for the MVP.
- No app to open on your side, nothing to scan or log. Trust the screen.

**Handing a sticker**
- EN: "Nice! Here's your **{character}** — collect all 3!"
- ES: "¡Bien! Aquí está tu **{personaje}** — ¡junta los 3!"

**Pointing them to the next one**
- EN: "Beat a different keeper next time to get the other characters."
- ES: "La próxima vez juega contra otro portero para conseguir los otros personajes."

**Completing the set**
- EN: "You collected all 3 — here's today's Colattanini reward!"

**Cross‑sell (optional, friendly, never pushy)**

## Simple rules (keep it light)
- **One sticker per keeper, per customer, per visit** (owner can adjust). They get
  the other characters by playing the other keepers, ideally on return visits.
- It's a **low‑stakes freebie** — don't argue over edge cases; use good judgment.
- **No info collected.** Never ask for name, phone, or email. There's nothing to enter.
- The reward is **owner‑set for the day**. If unsure what today's reward is, ask the owner/lead.

## Edge cases
- **"I already played at home / different screen":** fine — if they show a valid
  result screen in‑store, give the matching sticker (owner can limit to in‑store only).
- **Lost their collector sheet:** give a fresh sheet; stickers they still have can be re‑stuck. Don't track it.
- **Re‑scans / replays the same keeper:** they already have that character — point
  them to a different keeper. One of each is the goal.
- **Didn't score / lost the match:** still counts — playing the keeper is enough.
- **Asks what the prize is:** "Today's Colattanini reward" — give whatever the owner set for today.

## Daily reset / restock
- At open, check sticker + sheet stock; flag the owner to reorder when a character runs low.
- Track popularity by simply **noticing which stickers run out first** (no system needed).

## Hard "don'ts"
- Don't promise points, accounts, an app, or that we save their data — **we don't**.
- Don't require a purchase to play or to get a sticker.
- Don't name a specific prize unless the owner approved it for today.
