# Colattanini — How & Where to Print

_Plain-English guide for getting the album page + stickers printed and given away._

> **Status: PROTOTYPE run.** These are for a first physical proof, not a
> production order. The official art is ~1080 px (slightly soft at full page) —
> good enough to evaluate in hand. For a sharp production run later, regenerate
> the art ≥2500 px with `PROMPTS/COLATTANINI_PRINT_PROMPTS.md` and re-export.
> Canonical numbers: **CHURRO LATTE #10 · CAPPUCCINO #7 · MATCHA LEMONADE #9**
> (stickers match the album slots).

## Quick path — Office Depot (US, prototype)

Use the **Letter (8.5×11)** PDFs.

**See them in person:** officedepot.com → **Print & Copy → upload file → In-Store
Pickup** at your local store; or bring the PDFs on a phone/USB to the **Print &
Copy desk**. **Ask for ONE proof of each first**, check it, then run the rest.

| Piece | File | Ask for |
|-------|------|---------|
| **Album** (cardboard) | `colattanini-album-strikers-letter.pdf` | Single-sided **color on heavy cardstock** (~100 lb cover / ~270 gsm), **matte/uncoated**, Letter. Cut to the trim marks (or leave full Letter). |
| **Stickers** (labels) | `colattanini-sticker-sheet-letter.pdf` | Full color on **full-sheet white label/sticker paper**, Letter, matte or gloss. Office Depot won't kiss-cut — it prints as one label sheet; **cut each sticker with scissors** along the white gaps. |

_(A4 versions of both are also in this folder for printing outside the US. For
true peel-off kiss-cut stickers, an online sticker service — see below — does
that; Office Depot is fine for the prototype.)_

---

You have **two separate print products**:
1. **The album page** — a printed sheet customers collect stickers onto.
2. **The stickers** — peel-and-stick, given away with orders.

All files are in `PRINT/colattanini/` (PNG for preview, **PDF for the printer**).

---

## 1. The stickers (the giveaway)

**Best format: a "kiss-cut sticker sheet."** Multiple stickers sit on one backing
sheet; the customer peels each one off. Cheapest per unit and easy to hand out.

- **File to upload:** `colattanini-sticker-sheet.pdf` (A4, 6 stickers = 2 of each).
- **Where (online, easiest):** Sticker Mule (search "Sticker Sheets"), StickerApp,
  StickerYou, or UPrinting. Upload the PDF, choose **A4 / Letter sheet**, **kiss-cut**,
  white vinyl, **gloss or matte laminate**.
- **Where (local):** any sign/print shop or FedEx Office — ask for "kiss-cut sticker
  sheets, full color, laminated."
- **Want single die-cut stickers instead?** Use the individual files
  `sticker-10/11/12-*.png`. For a die-cut (cut to the rounded card shape) tell the
  printer "die-cut to the card outline."

**Order quantity:** these are cheapest in bulk (250–1000 sheets). Start with a small
proof order (25–50) to check color, then bulk-order.

---

## 2. The album page (the collector sheet)

- **File to upload:** `colattanini-album-strikers.pdf` (A4, 300 DPI, 3 mm bleed,
  trim marks included).
- **Print as a "flyer" on cardstock.** Where: Vistaprint / GotPrint / UPrinting
  (choose **A4 flyer, cardstock ~250–300 gsm, full color, single-sided**), or any
  local print shop. Hand the trim marks to a local shop so they cut to size.
- Customers stick the 3 stickers into the matching numbered slots.

---

## What to tell any printer (specs cheat-sheet)

| Item | Spec |
|------|------|
| Album page | A4 (210×297 mm), 300 DPI, full color, **bleed 3 mm** (already in file), cardstock 250–300 gsm |
| Sticker sheet | A4 kiss-cut, white vinyl, gloss/matte laminate, 300 DPI |
| Color | Files are RGB; most digital printers convert to CMYK fine. If they ask for CMYK, tell me and I'll export CMYK PDFs. |
| Cut | Album: cut to trim marks. Stickers: kiss-cut on the grey guides (or die-cut to the card shape). |

---

## Recommended easiest path
1. **Stickers:** upload `colattanini-sticker-sheet.pdf` to **Sticker Mule → Sticker
   Sheets**, kiss-cut, matte. Order a small proof first.
2. **Album:** upload `colattanini-album-strikers.pdf` to a flyer service (or local
   shop) on cardstock.
3. Give the album with a first order; drop a sticker sheet in every order until the
   set is collected.

If a printer needs a different size, CMYK, separate cut-lines, or a specific
template, send me their requirement and I'll export exactly that.
