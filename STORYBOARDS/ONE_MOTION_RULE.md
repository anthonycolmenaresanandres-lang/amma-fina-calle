# One Motion Rule

## Purpose

Keep generated scenes readable, premium, and controllable.

## Rule

Each shot gets one primary motion idea.

Examples:

- Steam rising from coffee.
- Camera slowly pushes toward the counter.
- A sticker peels onto a bag.
- A character turns their head toward a sign.
- A gold trail moves from one tower to another.

Do not combine multiple competing motion ideas in the same shot.

## Why

One motion keeps AI video/image prompts stable, reduces visual chaos, and makes future animation easier to implement with lightweight assets.

## Prompt Block

```txt
ONE MOTION ONLY:
Primary motion: [single action]
No secondary action, no extra camera spin, no extra particles, no unrelated movement.
```
