# Encanto Fina Calle Digital Menu Launch Handoff

## Strategic Shift

Shift from `Head of Household` promo framing to `Encanto Fina Calle Digital Menu Launch`.

The campaign should feel authoritative, sophisticated, welcoming, and mobile-first. It should introduce the Colattao Digital Menu and Cafe Rush as a premium Fina Calle OS experience.

## Owner As Primary Authority/Spokesperson

The owner is the primary authority/spokesperson. The owner identity lock is required before rendering any owner-led campaign video.

Use:

- `CHARACTER_LIBRARY/COLATTAO_OWNER_IDENTITY_LOCK_TEMPLATE.md`

Do not invent owner face, build, wardrobe, or expression details before approved references are provided.

## Adaptable Line

The previous concept line may be adapted only if brand appropriate:

"They don't talk about Bruno, but they do talk about Colattao's new game, Cafe Rush."

Use only if it supports the final campaign voice and does not cheapen the brand.

## Brand Voice

- Authoritative.
- Sophisticated.
- Welcoming.
- Warm cafe confidence.
- Premium local business energy.

## Production Requirements

- Mobile-first 9:16 production.
- Storyboard before generation.
- Owner identity lock required before rendering.
- Cafe environment lock required before rendering.
- Logos/text/QR overlays only.
- Digital Menu terminology only.
- No website mentions.
- Approved Digital Menu assets only.
- Separation of concerns workflow.

## Separation Of Concerns Workflow

1. Owner identity lock.
2. Cafe environment lock.
3. Storyboard.
4. Shot matrix.
5. Prompt packet.
6. Micro-motion test.
7. Render batch.
8. QA review.
9. Edit assembly.
10. Brand overlay.
11. Final export.

## Overlay Rule

Logos, text, QR codes, menu words, and brand marks must be added as static PNG/vector overlays in final editing. Never ask image or video models to generate readable brand text.

## Digital Menu Rule

Use Digital Menu, never website, in campaign language.

## Menu Asset Rule

Approved Digital Menu visuals must come from approved assets only. Do not let AI invent menu layouts.
