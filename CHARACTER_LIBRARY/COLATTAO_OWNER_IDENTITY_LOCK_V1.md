## B. Owner Visual Profile

Use this profile as stable wording only. Do not treat it as a final approved identity lock until the limitations below are resolved.

### Apparent Age Range

Young adult to early-middle adult appearance, approximately late 20s to 30s.

### Complexion / Skin Tone

Warm light-to-medium complexion with a natural glow. Avoid making the skin extremely pale, overly tan, gray, waxy, or plastic-smooth.

### Face Shape

Soft oval-to-heart face shape with balanced proportions and gently defined cheek structure.

### Jawline / Chin

Softly defined jawline with a tapered, delicate chin. Avoid square, heavy, overly angular, or exaggerated model-like jawlines.

### Eyebrows

Dark, well-shaped brows with a natural arch. The brows read polished but not overly sharp or artificial.

### Eyes

Almond-shaped, expressive eyes with a composed, attentive look. Eye color is not confidently locked from the available references; avoid inventing a bright or unusual eye color.

### Nose

Straight, refined nose profile with a slim bridge and natural proportions. Avoid making the nose overly small, upturned, wide, or sharply stylized.

### Lips

Full, defined lips with a soft cupid's bow. References often show a polished lip color, including red lipstick in some shots.

### Hair Color

Dark brown hair.

### Hair Texture

Smooth to softly wavy texture. Keep the hair natural and polished, not heavily curled or voluminous.

### Hairstyle

Short bob to chin/shoulder-length cut, usually side-parted or softly swept to one side. The hair frames the face with a refined, modern shape.

### Smile / Expression Tendencies

Composed, warm, confident expression. Often subtle or softly smiling rather than exaggerated. The default expression should feel welcoming and poised.

### Overall Build / Body Impression

Slim-to-average build with a polished, upright presence. Full-body references show a feminine silhouette and deliberate styling.

### Posture / Presence

Poised, confident, and composed. Reads as a polished local-business spokesperson with calm authority rather than a casual model pose.

### Style / Aesthetic Impression

Elegant, warm, premium, and fashion-conscious. References show a refined hospitality/spokesperson energy with coordinated outfits, warm tones, and polished presentation.

### Recurring Accessories / Styling Cues

Visible references include polished makeup, defined lip color, small earrings or ear jewelry in profile, necklace in the side-profile image, and a clutch/handbag in the full-body reference. Accessories should remain tasteful and restrained.

### Avoid Replacing With

Avoid these drift-prone substitutions unless a new approved identity version is created:

- Do not replace `dark brown short bob` with long blonde hair, long black hair, heavy curls, pixie cut, or high ponytail.
- Do not replace `soft oval-to-heart face shape` with square face, round face, sharply angular model face, or exaggerated cheekbones.
- Do not replace `warm light-to-medium complexion` with very pale porcelain, deep bronze, gray, waxy, or plastic skin.
- Do not replace `composed warm confident expression` with exaggerated grin, stern corporate headshot, cartoon smile, or glamour-model expression.
- Do not replace `elegant premium local-business spokesperson energy` with influencer, runway model, generic barista, or anonymous customer energy.

### Forbidden Synonym Drift

- `Owner/spokesperson` should not become `random cafe customer`.
- `Premium local-business spokesperson` should not become `fashion model`.
- `Short bob` should not become `long flowing hair`.
- `Warm confident expression` should not become `overly dramatic expression`.
