# Negative Identity Protocol

## Purpose

Define what a character, brand, or client visual must never become. This prevents AI from drifting into generic, over-polished, wrong-culture, wrong-age, or off-brand outputs.

## Negative Identity Fields

Document:

- Not this age range.
- Not this ethnicity/cultural read.
- Not this wardrobe style.
- Not this body type or posture.
- Not cartoon unless requested.
- Not corporate stock-photo energy.
- Not generic startup aesthetic.
- Not fake logos or invented marks.
- Not luxury cliches unless the brand specifically uses them.

## Prompt Block

```txt
NEGATIVE IDENTITY:
Do not generate: [wrong identities, wrong style, wrong era, wrong wardrobe]
Do not alter: [protected character traits]
Do not include: AI-generated logo, invented brand mark, fake signage, fake claims.
```

## Review Rule

Reject an asset if it is technically attractive but weakens identity consistency. Brand consistency beats novelty.
