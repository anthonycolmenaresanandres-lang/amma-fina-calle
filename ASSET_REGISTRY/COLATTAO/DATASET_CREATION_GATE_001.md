# Colattao Dataset Creation Gate 001

## Purpose

This gate defines the minimum capture checklist required before Colattao campaign asset ingestion, owner identity locking, cafe environment locking, storyboarding, or AI generation. Assets must be approved, marked pending, rejected, deprecated, or intentionally waived before campaign production begins.

## 1. Owner Photo Requirements

Required owner/spokesperson references:

- Front portrait.
- 3/4 portrait.
- Left profile if possible.
- Right profile if possible.
- Full-body standing.
- Neutral expression.
- Warm owner/spokesperson expression.
- Same outfit across all shots.
- Good lighting.
- No filters.
- No sunglasses.
- No heavy background clutter.

Approval notes:

- Owner references must be provided by Anthony or the client.
- Owner references remain pending until explicitly approved.
- Owner identity lock cannot begin from screenshots, low-quality crops, or AI-generated substitutes.

## 2. Cafe Environment Requirements

Required cafe references:

- Counter reference.
- Interior wide shot.
- Menu/display area.
- Lighting reference.
- Owner standing position.
- Customer standing position.
- Approved vertical 9:16 camera angles.

Approval notes:

- Cafe references must show the real Colattao environment or an explicitly approved campaign environment.
- Cafe environment lock cannot begin from unrelated cafe inspiration images unless they are clearly marked as references only.
- Vertical 9:16 camera angles must be approved before short-form campaign generation.

## 3. Digital Menu Requirements

Required Digital Menu references:

- Mobile screenshot.
- Clean crop.
- No browser clutter if possible.
- Approved route/destination written clearly.

Approval notes:

- Use the term Digital Menu, not website.
- Digital Menu screenshots must be approved before campaign generation.
- Routes and destinations must be written clearly before QR overlays are produced.

## 4. Overlay Requirements

Required overlay assets:

- Colattao logo PNG/SVG.
- Fina Calle logo PNG/SVG if used.
- QR code PNG.
- CTA text as editable overlay, never AI-generated.

Overlay rules:

- Logos, QR codes, text, and menu copy are overlays only.
- AI-generated logos are rejected.
- AI-generated QR codes are rejected.
- AI-generated CTA text inside an image render is rejected.
- Final CTA text must remain editable in the campaign composition layer.

## 5. Rejection Rules

Reject assets that are:

- Blurry.
- Filtered.
- Low-light.
- Distorted.
- AI-generated text.
- AI-generated logos.
- AI-generated QR codes.
- Inconsistent outfit across owner reference shots.
- Wrong brand terminology.
- Cropped so tightly that identity, posture, or environment context is unusable.
- Watermarked or unclear in source ownership.

## 6. Approval Gate

No owner identity lock, cafe environment lock, storyboard, or AI generation may begin until required assets are approved or intentionally waived.

Required gate decision before campaign production:

- Owner references: approved or waived.
- Cafe references: approved or waived.
- Digital Menu screenshot: approved or waived.
- Overlay assets: approved or waived.
- QR destinations: verified or intentionally deferred.

If a required asset is waived, the waiver must state:

- What is being waived.
- Who approved the waiver.
- Why production may continue without it.
- What risk remains.
- What replacement asset is expected later.
