## 3. Decision Options

### Option 1: Approve As-Is

Use this if:

- The 9:16 and 4:5 layouts feel premium enough for internal/client preview.
- The recovered logo candidate is acceptable for preview use.
- The QR size and placement are acceptable.
- The CTA is approved as written.

Outcome:

- Move to `Static Mockup Finalization Pass 001`.

### Option 2: Approve With Minor Edits

Use this if:

- The overall direction is correct but a small adjustment is needed.
- Likely minor edits: logo size, QR scale, bottom panel spacing, CTA line break, or gold border intensity.

Outcome:

- Perform a narrow revision pass without rebuilding the full concept direction.

### Option 3: Revise Layout

Use this if:

- The product crop, QR panel, or logo placement feels visually heavy.
- The 9:16 concept needs more breathing room.
- The 4:5 concept needs a different crop or less bottom-panel weight.

Outcome:

- Create `Static Mockup Revision Pass 001` with explicit layout requirements.

### Option 4: Reject And Rebuild

Use this if:

- The concept does not meet the premium Colattao direction.
- The recovered logo is not acceptable even for preview.
- The QR/CTA treatment feels too much like a generic flyer.

Outcome:

- Return to assembly planning and rebuild with a different visual structure.

## 4. Recommended Decision

Recommended decision: **approve with minor edits before client preview**.

Reasoning:

- The product-first direction aligns with the Premium Visual Mood Board and keeps Colattao feeling warm, premium, and restrained.
- QR scan functionality has passed and destination is confirmed as Colattao Digital Menu.
- The concept is strong enough as a direction, but the recovered Colattao logo candidates still need visual approval because they inherit rough/speckled edges from the source image.
- The 9:16 and 4:5 layouts should receive a final phone-readability review before showing externally.

Do not treat this as final production approval yet.
