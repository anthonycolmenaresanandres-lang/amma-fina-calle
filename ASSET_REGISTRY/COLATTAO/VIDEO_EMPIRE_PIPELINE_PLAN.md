# Colattao Video Empire Pipeline Plan

## Purpose

This plan defines the long-term Fina Calle OS media pipeline for Colattao Encanto Launch and future campaign/video production. The goal is to create repeatable short-form campaigns while protecting character consistency, brand consistency, Digital Menu terminology, logo integrity, QR integrity, and budget discipline.

This is planning only. No live provider calls are authorized.

## Core Doctrine

- Asset approval comes before generation.
- Identity lock comes before owner/spokesperson content.
- Environment lock comes before cafe scene generation.
- Storyboard comes before provider spend.
- Shot matrix comes before batch generation.
- Overlay assembly comes after generation.
- Logos, QR codes, CTA text, and Digital Menu text are overlays only.

## Pipeline Stages

### 1. Asset Approval

Inputs:

- Approved logos.
- Approved QR destinations and scan-tested QR images.
- Approved Digital Menu screenshots or visual panels.
- Approved owner references, if owner appears.
- Approved cafe references, if environment is specific.
- Approved product/drink/sticker assets.

Outputs:

- Asset approval packet.
- Missing asset list.
- Waiver list for anything intentionally skipped.

### 2. Identity Lock

Required for owner/spokesperson clips.

Inputs:

- Front portrait.
- 3/4 portrait.
- Profile references if available.
- Full-body standing reference.
- Neutral expression.
- Warm expression.
- Same outfit.

Outputs:

- Identity lock summary.
- Negative identity rules.
- Use limits.

### 3. Environment Lock

Required for real cafe scene continuity.

Inputs:

- Counter reference.
- Interior wide shot.
- Display/menu area.
- Lighting reference.
- Owner standing position.
- Customer standing position.
- Approved 9:16 camera angles.

Outputs:

- Environment lock summary.
- Blocking map.
- Forbidden environment drift list.

### 4. Storyboard

Define each short-form clip as 3 to 5 beats:

- Hook.
- Product or Digital Menu reveal.
- Brand proof.
- CTA overlay.
- End-card.

No storyboard may rely on AI-generated readable text.

### 5. Shot Matrix

For each shot:

- Shot id.
- Duration.
- Frame ratio.
- Subject.
- Camera move.
- One Motion Rule.
- Z-Axis Blocking Rule.
- Source assets.
- Overlay assets.
- Provider target.
- Estimated cost.
- QA checklist.

### 6. Prompt Packet

Prompt packets must include:

- Approved source assets.
- Character identity lock, if applicable.
- Negative identity protocol.
- Environment lock, if applicable.
- Camera and motion rules.
- Forbidden logo/text/QR generation rules.
- Output count.
- Cost estimate.

### 7. Micro-Motion Test

Before batch generation:

- Generate 1 low-cost movement test if provider spend is approved.
- Validate identity, environment, product shape, and motion restraint.
- Reject if text/logos/QR are hallucinated.

### 8. Batch Generation

Batch only after micro-motion passes.

Batch rules:

- Small batches first.
- One campaign concept at a time.
- No more than approved output count.
- Stop if consistency degrades.

### 9. QA

Review for:

- Owner likeness consistency.
- Cafe/environment consistency.
- Product shape consistency.
- No generated logos.
- No generated QR codes.
- No generated Digital Menu text.
- No generated CTA text.
- No wrong terminology.
- Mobile readability.

### 10. Overlay Assembly

Use CapCut or equivalent for:

- Logo overlays.
- QR overlays.
- CTA overlays.
- Digital Menu proof overlays.
- Captions.
- End-card.
- Audio/voice if approved.

### 11. Final Export

Required final packet:

- 9:16 final video.
- 4:5 crop if needed.
- Static thumbnail.
- Overlay source file.
- Registry records.
- QA notes.
- Approval status.

## Campaign Types

### Short-Form 9:16 Ad

Best for launch awareness, QR scanning, and Digital Menu promotion.

### Owner Spokesperson Clip

Use only after owner identity lock is approved.

### Product/Drink Reveal Clip

Use product stickers, product stills, and simple camera motion.

### Digital Menu Launch Clip

Use approved Digital Menu proof and overlay CTA.

### Cafe Rush Promo Clip

Use Cafe Rush CTA overlay, game visuals, and approved game route QR.

### Future Cinematic Client Campaign

Reuse the same pipeline with a client-specific asset packet.

- CapCut or equivalent: final assembly and overlays.
