## Recommended First Composition

### 9:16 Product-First Static Mockup

Layer order:

1. Base image: coffee + pastry 9:16 product hero.
2. Subtle top safe area reserved for logo overlay, if approved.
3. Middle visual focus: cup and pastry remain unobstructed.
4. Lower safe area reserved for Digital Menu CTA and QR overlay, if approved.
5. Footer/signature overlay only if approved and legible.

Do not embed text, QR, pricing, logo, menu copy, or legal copy inside the visual asset.

## Overlay Zones

9:16 overlay zones:

- Top 8-12 percent: logo or short campaign mark only.
- Center 60 percent: visual subject only; avoid overlays across product or owner face.
- Bottom 18-24 percent: CTA, QR, and optional signature overlay.

4:5 overlay zones:

- Top 10 percent: optional logo overlay.
- Center 70 percent: product/environment subject.
- Bottom 15-20 percent: CTA/QR only if scan-tested and legible.

## Mobile Readability Rules

- Product must remain recognizable at phone size.
- QR must never sit on busy product texture unless scan-tested.
- CTA text must be editable overlay copy, not generated into an image.
- Digital Menu proof must be an approved screenshot or approved proof crop.
- Keep one primary message per frame.
- Use restrained contrast: espresso, cream, muted gold, and warm amber.

## Premium QA Checklist

- Brand prestige feels high-end and curated.
- Visual consistency fits Colattao's warm espresso/amber mood.
- Environment detail supports the cafe identity.
- Product remains appetizing and clearly visible.
- Overlay zones are clean and mobile-readable.
- No generated readable logo, QR, CTA, pricing, legal, Digital Menu copy, or menu copy appears.
- Owner identity is not finalized or overused.
- B001-A13 is not used as final hero without review.

## Missing Blockers

- Approved logo overlay.
- Scan-tested Digital Menu QR PNG.
- Scan-tested Cafe Rush QR PNG if used.
- Approved mobile Digital Menu screenshot/proof.
- Final CTA text as editable overlay copy.
- True transparent owner cutout if owner appears prominently.
- Cleaner owner front and 3/4 portraits before final spokesperson generation.
