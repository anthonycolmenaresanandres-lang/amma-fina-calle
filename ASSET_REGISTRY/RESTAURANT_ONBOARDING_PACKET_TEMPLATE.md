# Restaurant Onboarding Packet Template

## Purpose

This packet captures the minimum assets and business context required to translate the Fina Calle premium campaign system to a restaurant or venue.

No AI generation should begin until required assets are approved or intentionally waived.

## Logo Assets

Required:

- Logo PNG with transparent background.
- Logo SVG if available.
- Alternate light/dark versions if available.
- Brand color notes.

Rules:

- Logos are overlays only.
- AI-generated logos are rejected.
- Logo must not be regenerated or visually mutated.

## Owner Photo Packet

Required if owner/spokesperson appears:

- Front portrait.
- 3/4 portrait.
- Left/right profile if possible.
- Full-body standing.
- Neutral expression.
- Warm owner/spokesperson expression.
- Same outfit across all shots.
- Good lighting.
- No filters.
- No sunglasses.
- No heavy background clutter.

## Interior / Environment Packet

Required if venue-specific environment appears:

- Exterior if relevant.
- Interior wide shot.
- Counter or host stand.
- Menu/display area.
- Lighting reference.
- Owner standing position.
- Customer standing position.
- Approved vertical 9:16 camera angles.

## Digital Menu Assets

Required:

- Mobile Digital Menu screenshot.
- Clean crop without browser clutter if possible.
- Approved route/destination written clearly.
- Approved menu sections or proof visuals.

Rules:

- Use Digital Menu terminology for Fina Calle menu experiences.
- Digital Menu text is not AI-generated.
- Menu copy and prices are not invented.

## Product Hero Images

Required:

- 3 to 6 food/drink hero items.
- Product names.
- Product priority ranking.
- Approved product photos or stickers.
- Seasonal product notes if relevant.

## QR / CTA Destinations

Required:

- QR PNG.
- Destination URL.
- Scan-test result.
- CTA text as editable overlay.

Rules:

- QR codes are overlays only.
- AI-generated QR codes are rejected.
- CTA text is overlay only.

## Heritage / Cultural Notes

Capture:

- Cultural origin or story.
- Family/owner story if approved.
- Ingredients or product traditions.
- Words, symbols, colors, or references to avoid.
- Approved cues that can appear visually.

## Audience Notes

Capture:

- Customer type.
- Neighborhood context.
- Buying moment.
- Tone: elegant, warm, bold, family, nightlife, luxury, casual premium, etc.
- What the audience should do next.

## Premium Positioning Notes

Capture:

- Why the restaurant feels premium.
- What should be elevated visually.
- What should remain authentic and not over-polished.
- What campaign style would feel wrong.
