## Physical spec

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Shape | Die‑cut, rounded (character silhouette or rounded square) |
| Size | **2 in (≈50 mm)** on the long edge |
| Material | Vinyl, matte or gloss; **weatherproof/peelable** |
| Finish | Matte preferred (less glare under café lights) |
| Bleed / safe area | **3 mm bleed**, keep art **3 mm inside** the cut line |
| Color | CMYK for print; keep a digital RGB master |
| Backing | Easy‑peel; suitable for sticking onto a laminated sheet |

## Art direction
- **Soccer + café mash‑up mascots**, friendly and collectible (Panini‑style energy,
  but **original** — see brand guardrails below).
- Each character carries a small **icon of its signature item** (churro/latte, coco
  drink, croissant‑sandwich) so the cross‑sell reads at a glance.
- Use the Colattao palette (warm cream/coffee/gold). Tie each character loosely to
  its keeper's difficulty feel (Street = playful, Club = sporty, Pro = champion).
- Include the small **"Colattanini"** wordmark + a tiny **#1 / #2 / #3** collectible number.

## File deliverables (per character)
- Vector master (`.ai` or `.svg`)
- Transparent `.png` at ≥ 1024 px (digital use / collector sheet art)
- Print‑ready PDF with bleed + crop marks

## Quantities (starting run — owner adjusts)
- Print in **equal sets** (1 : 1 : 1) so customers can complete the collection.
- Suggested first run: enough complete sets for ~1–2 weeks of traffic; reorder by
  counting what's handed out (manual — no tracking system).

## Brand / legal guardrails (must follow)
Per `PRODUCT_MODULES/GAME_CUSTOMIZATION_PROTOCOL.md`:
- **Original characters only.** No FIFA / World Cup / club logos, no real player
  likenesses, no real human faces, no trademarked badges or official ball designs.
- Colattao‑owned art; client approves all sticker art **in writing before printing**.
- No prices or unverified claims printed on the stickers.
