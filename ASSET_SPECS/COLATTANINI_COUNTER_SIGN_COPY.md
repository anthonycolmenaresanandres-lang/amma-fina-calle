# Colattanini — Counter Sign / Table Tent Copy
_Asset spec · MVP signage copy (bilingual) · Created 2026-06-07_

Copy for the **counter sign / table tent** that explains the campaign and shows
the QR. Copy only — design separately. Manual campaign — see
`PRODUCT_MODULES/COLATTAO_COLATTANINI_COLLECTIBLE_CAMPAIGN.md`.

> Keep the reward as **"today's Colattanini reward"** until the exact prize is approved.

---

## Recommended sign layout (top → bottom)

1. **Headline**
2. **The 3 characters row** (small art + names)
3. **3 steps (how to play)**
4. **QR code** + "Scan to play"
5. **Reward line** + small print

---

## EN copy

**Headline:** Play. Collect. Win a Colattanini.
**Sub:** Free penalty‑shootout game — collect all 3 Colattanini characters.

**Characters:**
- **Churro Chispa** — beat the Street Keeper
- **Coco Beach Blitzer** — beat the Club Keeper
- **Cali Croissant Captain** — beat the Pro Keeper

**3 steps:**
1. **Scan** the QR and play a keeper.
2. **Show your result screen** to our team.
3. **Grab your Colattanini sticker.**

**QR callout:** Scan to play — no app, no sign‑up.
**Reward line:** Collect all 3 → **today's Colattanini reward.**
**Small print:** One game, on your phone. We don't collect any personal info.

---

## ES copy

**Título:** Juega. Colecciona. Gana un Colattanini.
**Sub:** Juego de penales gratis — junta los 3 personajes Colattanini.

**Personajes:**
- **Churro Chispa** — vence al Portero Calle (Street)
- **Coco Beach Blitzer** — vence al Portero Club
- **Cali Croissant Captain** — vence al Portero Pro

**3 pasos:**
1. **Escanea** el QR y juega contra un portero.
2. **Muestra tu pantalla de resultado** a nuestro equipo.
3. **Llévate tu sticker Colattanini.**

**Texto del QR:** Escanea para jugar — sin app, sin registro.
**Recompensa:** Junta los 3 → **la recompensa Colattanini de hoy.**
**Letra pequeña:** Un juego, en tu teléfono. No recogemos datos personales.

## Cross‑sell line (optional, owner's choice — never required to play)

## Guardrails
- Do not name a specific prize; use the reward wording above.
- Do not imply accounts, tracking, points, or that a purchase is required to play.
- QR must point to the live Penalty Shootout game; verify on a phone before printing.
- Use only the 3 verified menu items; no others.
