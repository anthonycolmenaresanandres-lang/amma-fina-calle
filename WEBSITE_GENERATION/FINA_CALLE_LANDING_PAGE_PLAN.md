# Fina Calle Landing Page Plan

## 1. Purpose of the Landing Page

The Fina Calle landing page should introduce AMMA Ventures LLC DBA Fina Calle as a premium builder of interactive digital storefronts for local businesses. The page should make the offer understandable in one visit: Fina Calle turns ordinary local business touchpoints into branded, mobile-first digital experiences.

The landing page is not a full platform, dashboard, or checkout flow. Its purpose is to explain the service, show proof through Colattao, and move interested business owners toward a project conversation.

## 2. Target Customer

The primary customer is a local business owner who wants their brand to feel more premium without taking on unnecessary technical complexity.

Initial ideal customers include:

- Cafes and coffee shops
- Restaurants and quick-service food businesses
- Barbershops and beauty businesses
- Gyms and local fitness studios
- Boutique retail or service businesses with QR-driven customer touchpoints

The buyer likely cares about presentation, customer engagement, speed, and differentiation more than technical details.

## 3. Core Message

Fina Calle turns local businesses into interactive digital brands.

Supporting message:

Interactive storefronts can include QR menus, mini-games, digital storefront pages, owner intake systems, branded campaign assets, and modular client experiences that grow over time.

## 4. Page Sections

Recommended first version:

- Hero: Fina Calle, core message, short explanation, primary CTA
- What We Build: digital QR menus, mini-games, website concepts, owner request intake, QR signage/sticker campaigns, future command centers
- Flagship Case Study: Colattao Cafe Rush
- Packages Preview: offer categories without fixed pricing
- Founder Pilot: early-client positioning and benefit
- Philosophy: mobile-first, modular, low-liability, POS-separated
- Contact / Start a Project: simple project inquiry CTA

## 5. Offer Architecture Without Fixed Prices

The landing page should present offer types without publishing fixed prices yet.

Offer categories:

- Starter Digital Menu
- Interactive Menu Plus
- Game Experience Package
- Full Digital Storefront
- Seasonal Campaign Add-On
- Founder Pilot Build

Pricing language should stay flexible:

- Custom quote
- Pilot availability limited
- Scope depends on business type, assets, and desired modules

Avoid publishing rigid pricing until fulfillment time, scope, and repeatable package boundaries are proven.

## 6. Colattao as Case Study

Colattao should be positioned as the flagship proof of concept.

The case study should show that Fina Calle can combine:

- A playable mobile cafe game
- A digital menu
- A website concept
- Owner-facing presentation pages
- Request intake
- Branded visual systems

The landing page should link to the live Colattao experience but avoid implying that every future client receives the exact same build. Colattao is the reference implementation, not the only product shape.

## 7. Founder Pilot Positioning

Founder Pilot should frame early clients as select launch partners.

Positioning:

- Built directly with the founder
- More custom attention than future standardized packages
- Helps shape the Fina Calle product system
- Limited availability
- Best for businesses willing to move fast and give feedback

The Founder Pilot should feel premium and selective, not discounted or experimental.

## 8. No-Liability Payment Philosophy

The landing page should clearly avoid payment-processing claims for client businesses.

Principles:

- No unnecessary payment liability
- Client POS stays separate
- Fina Calle does not replace the client checkout system in early builds
- Future AMMA/Fina Calle billing can use hosted payment links for Fina Calle client invoices
- No Stripe SDK, Square SDK, cart, checkout, stored cards, or customer payment handling in the landing page

This protects both Fina Calle and the client while the product system matures.

## 9. Future CTAs

Early CTA:

- Start a Project

Future CTA options:

- Apply for Founder Pilot
- View Colattao Case Study
- Request a Digital Menu
- Build My Interactive Storefront
- Talk to Fina Calle

The first version can use a simple `mailto:` link. Future versions may use a lightweight inquiry form, but only after intake structure is ready.

## 10. What Not to Build Yet

Do not build these into the first landing page:

- Login
- Dashboard
- Client portal
- Payment processing
- Checkout
- Stripe or Square SDKs
- Backend/API routes
- Fake analytics
- Fake customer counts
- Overpromised automation
- Complex package configurator
- Public pricing table with fixed prices
- Claims that Fina Calle replaces client POS systems

The first version should stay focused: premium positioning, clear offers, Colattao proof, and a simple project CTA.
